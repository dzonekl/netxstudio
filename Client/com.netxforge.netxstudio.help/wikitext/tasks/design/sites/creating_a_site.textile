h1. Creating or Editing a Site

When creating or editing a Site the following screen will appear.

!PLUGINS_ROOT/com.netxforge.netxstudio.help/img/tasks/design/sites/creating_a_site.png!

The following data should be entered 

*General Information*

* The Site Name
* The Site Region
* The Site Area
* The Site City
* The Site Street
* The Site Building Nr

*Geo Spatial Information*

*The Site longitude
*The Site latitude

"According to the Geographic Coordinate System":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system

*Cancel* => Click on the 'Back' link at the bottom of the screen.
*Save* => Click on the 'Save link at the bottom of the screen.
