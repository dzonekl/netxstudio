h1. Creating or Editing a Warehouse

When creating or editing a Warehouse the following screen will appear.

!PLUGINS_ROOT/com.netxforge.netxstudio.help/img/tasks/design/warehouses/creating_a_warehouse.png!

The following data should be entered 

* The Warehouse Name
* The Warehouse Description

*Cancel* => Click on the 'Back' link at the bottom of the screen.
*Save* => Click on the 'Save link at the bottom of the screen.
